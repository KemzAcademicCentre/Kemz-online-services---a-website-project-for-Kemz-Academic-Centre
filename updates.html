Online Classes, 2025

The school Online Classes Platform is now active and our first classes will be commencing this coming holiday starting on 8th to 31st December, 2025. These will be form 1 classes for now and will be conducted following the schedule (time table) displaying on the Online Classes platform. They will be conducted for free for the meantime and all form 1 learners are free to join including those who are not part of Kemz Academic Centre. For easy coordination, you will be required to join a Whatsapp group in advance for Online Learning where Meeting IDs / Passcodes will be posted which have to be used to join online classes once they commence. To join this special Whatsapp group, you can contact / or Whatsapp the admin on: +260977819249
