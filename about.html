History of Our Institution

Kemz Academic Centre is a Private School whose founder is Mr Mayungo Kennedy, a teacher by profession who holds a bachelor's degree from the University of Africa.

The school was established in the year 2005. The school started as a Pre-School and Lower Primary School with just 15 learners. KEMZ Academic Centre has grown exponentially over the years with an expansion that now includes Upper Primary Level and Secondary Ordinary Level at Lusaka's main campus. Today, we serve over 1000 learners across all educational levels at the main campus.

A few years ago, another campus was established in Mumbwa. The campus runs from Pre-School to Grade 7 and has over 300 learners.

Our growth is attributed to our commitment to academic excellence and our dedicated staff.
