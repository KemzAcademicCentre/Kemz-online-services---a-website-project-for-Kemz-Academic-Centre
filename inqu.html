⏰ What are the school hours?
The school operates from 07:20 hrs to 16:00 hrs.
📌 How do I register my child?
Click on the navigation menu on the top right corner and select option 7 (Registration). Fill in the registration form details and once done, click on submit.
📊 How can I access my child's results?
Click on the navigation menu icon on the top right corner, select option 8 (Results). Fill in details: student ID number, select term and select the year. Click on fetch for results to display. To download click on "Download PDF results slip".
👩‍🏫 How do I contact my child's teacher?
Contact the school manager on +260977819249 for assistance on the contact details of your child's teacher.
