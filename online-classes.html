How Online Classes Work

Our online classes are powered by Zoom, a reliable video conferencing platform. Here's how it works:

  1. Teachers schedule classes and receive unique meeting links
  2. Students join using the meeting ID or direct link
  3. No special software needed – works in web browsers
  4. Interactive features: screen sharing, virtual whiteboard, chat
  5. Recordings available for students who miss live sessions
  6. Teachers can also conduct virtual meetings using the same portal when provided with a meeting ID

Tips for Successful Online Classes

  1. Test your audio and video before joining
  2. Use a stable internet connection
  3. Join from a quiet, well-lit environment
  4. Mute your microphone when not speaking
  5. Use headphones for better audio quality
